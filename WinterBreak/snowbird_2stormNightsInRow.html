Snow Report

12-10-21 

Good Morning!

This is Jayme with your daily snow report for Friday, December 10. So far, the storm has over delivered and we have received 20 inches in the last 24 hours. Throughout the day we can expect 9 - 14 more inches calling for a powder day here in the Cottonwoods! We all owe it to our mountain operations teams who have been working day and night in efforts to get terrain open given what Mother Nature has provided so far. With that, we are hoping the new snow will set us up nicely for potential of new terrain opening. Please be sure to take caution when commuting in the canyon with traction devices ready and come on up to enjoy the snow! 

Gadzoom, Little Cloud and the Aerial Tram will be spinning from 9 am - 4 pm, conditions and weather permitting with no beginner terrain available. Please remember that early season conditions exist and unmarked hazards are present. We kindly advise you to ski and ride with caution and stay in control.

For hours of operation and daily terrain access, please see below or download the Snowbird App.

Happy skiing and riding!
